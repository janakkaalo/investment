Detailed Execution Plan

1. Small Cap Quant Funds

The Growth Engine

₹2.25 Lakhs

The Strategy: Do not buy manually. Use "Quant" funds that pick stocks using math and algorithms.

The Tactic: STP (Systematic Transfer Plan). Do not invest ₹2.25L at once. Put it in a Liquid Fund first, then automatically move ₹25,000/week into the Small Cap fund.

Action Items:

  • Select a Small Cap Momentum Fund/Smallcase.
  • Deposit lump sum into Liquid Fund.
  • Set Weekly Transfer of ₹25k for 9 weeks.

2. P2P & Invoice Discounting

The Income Generator

₹1.35 Lakhs

The Strategy: Be the bank. Lend money to individuals (P2P) or companies (Invoice Discounting) to earn interest.

The Tactic: Hyper-Diversification. Never lend ₹1L to one person. Split it into 200 loans of ₹500 each. If 5 people don't pay, you still make money.

Action Items:

  • Register on Cred/Lenden/Tyke.
  • Allocate ₹1L to P2P (Auto-Invest Mode).
  • Allocate ₹35k to Invoice Discounting (90-day lock).

3. Weekend Swing Trading

The Alpha Booster

₹90,000

The Strategy: Buy strong stocks that have temporarily fallen. Sell when they bounce back.

The Tactic: GTT (Good Till Triggered). You have a job, so don't watch the screen. Set automatic Buy and Sell orders on the weekend. The system executes them for you.

Action Items:

  • Open Discount Broker account (Zerodha/Groww).
  • Scan Nifty 500 on Saturdays.
  • Set GTT orders: Buy @ Support, Sell @ +10%.
