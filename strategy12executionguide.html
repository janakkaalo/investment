def buy_stock(symbol, qty):
    dhan.place_order(
        security_id=symbol,
        exchange_segment=dhan.NSE,
        transaction_type=dhan.BUY,
        quantity=qty,
        order_type=dhan.MARKET,
        product_type=dhan.INTRADAY,
        validity=dhan.DAY
    )

    print("OVERSOLD IN UPTREND: TRIGGER BUY")
    buy_stock('RELIANCE', 10)

    print("OVERBOUGHT: TRIGGER SELL")
    sell_stock('RELIANCE', 10)
PHASE 5: TAX EFFICIENCY

Keeping What You Earn

Making money is half the battle. Keeping it is the other half. The Sakuntala Protocol uses a specific combination of Indian Tax Laws to legally minimize tax liability on business income (Trading is considered Speculative/Non-Speculative Business Income).

1

Section 44AD (Presumptive Taxation)

Instead of meticulous bookkeeping, you declare 6% of your turnover as profit. For digital transactions, this is the standard.

2

Section 87A Rebate

If your total taxable income is below the threshold (₹7 Lakhs under New Regime, effectively), the government gives a rebate of up to ₹25,000, making tax ZERO.
