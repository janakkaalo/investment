Pillar II: The Kolkata Thermal Challenge

A 4-GPU rig generates 2000 Watts of heat. In a Kolkata summer (35°C+), a standard room will reach 50°C in minutes, destroying the hardware.

The Solution: "Hot Aisle Extraction"

  • Dedicated 2-Ton Inverter AC for Intake.
  • Sealed Rack Enclosure.
  • Industrial Inline Fan (800 CFM) pulling heat.
  • Direct exhaust vent out of the 3rd-floor window.
