The "Secret Sauce": Diversified Aggression

Most people put all their money in one basket (like FD or Gold). We are building a "Hybrid Engine" that has three parts: one for monthly cash, one for future wealth, and one for safety.

Your ‚Çπ4.5L Allocation Pie

1

The Workhorse: EV Fleet (FOCO)

What is it? You buy ~7 electric scooters. You don't ride them. You lease them to a company (like Zypp). They rent them to delivery drivers.

Why? It pays you monthly rent (like a house, but cheaper to buy).
Return: 22-25% per year.

2

The Multiplier: Small Cap Fund

What is it? You buy shares of small, fast-growing Indian companies via a Mutual Fund.

Why? While scooters lose value (depreciation), these shares gain value over 5-10 years.
Return: ~25-30% CAGR (Historic).

3

The Anchor: Invoice Discounting

What is it? You lend money to vendors who are waiting for payments from giants like Amazon/Flipkart.

Why? It's safer than stocks and pays better than FDs. Plus, you get your money back quickly (30-60 days).
Return: 12-13% Pre-tax.
